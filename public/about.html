What is BookExplorer?

BookExplorer is an interactive platform for book lovers to discover new books, explore detailed information about them, and manage their personal reading lists.

Features

    Search and discover books using the Open Library API.

    Create and manage your own personalized reading list.

    Explore author information and genre details.
